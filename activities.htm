Activities

Explore a range of enjoyable experiences during your stay.

Boating

Experience peaceful waters and stunning coastal views on a relaxing boating excursion.

Fishing

Enjoy fishing in our rich local waters — perfect for enthusiasts and beginners alike.

Swimming

Cool off and refresh yourself in our safe, scenic swimming spots.

Indigenous Awareness

Learn about the rich cultural heritage of the Ngarluma people and their deep connection to this land.

Dining

Savour local and international cuisine prepared with the freshest ingredients at our onsite restaurant.
